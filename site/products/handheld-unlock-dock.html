The Handheld Unlock Dock is a durable, portable magnetic unlocking station for Generation Faraday locking bags. Designed for mobility and frequent use, it features a comfortable built-in handle and non-slip pads for stability. It delivers the same fast, secure unlocking as the standard dock—perfect for schools, workplaces, and other environments requiring device management on the go.

  • Compact and Durable Design - Crafted from high-quality materials to withstand heavy use in busy settings.

Usage & Care

  1. Placement and Use - Place on a stable surface or firmly hold in hand.
  2. Unlocking a Bag - Hold the Generation Faraday Max Lock bag, align with the dock, and press until the lock releases.
  3. Cleaning - Wipe with a dry or slightly damp cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasives.
  4. Storage - Keep in a clean, dry area away from extreme heat or moisture.
