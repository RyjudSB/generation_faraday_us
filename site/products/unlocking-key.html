The Generation Faraday Unlocking Key is a compact, easy-to-use tool designed to unlock Generation Faraday Max Lock bags. The cap unscrews to reveal the magnetic unlocking tool, allowing for fast, controlled unlocking whenever needed. Built for repeated use, it provides administrators and teachers with an efficient way to manage locked bags on the go. Lightweight and portable, the Unlocking Key comes with a neck lanyard for easy access.

Specifications

  • Magnetic Unlocking Mechanism – Easily opens the locking mechanism on Generation Faraday locking bags.
  • Compact and Durable Design – Crafted from high-quality materials to withstand heavy use in busy settings.
  • Portable With Attached Lanyard – Handheld size unlocking tool with attached neck lanyard allows staff to easily carry and unlock bags anywhere on the go.
  • Protective Cap – Prevents unintended magnetic attraction to the Unlocking Key and unscrews easily to expose the magnetic side when in use.
  • Universal Compatibility – Designed to work seamlessly with all Generation Faraday locking bags equipped with the MAX-LOCK system.
  • SKU – GFUD-KEY

Usage & Care

  1. Remove Cap – Unscrew cap to access magnet.
  2. Unlocking a Bag – Hold the Generation Faraday Max Lock bag, press the Unlocking Key’s exposed magnet to the base of the bag’s magnetic lock until the lock releases.
  3. Cleaning – Wipe with a dry or slightly damp cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasives.
