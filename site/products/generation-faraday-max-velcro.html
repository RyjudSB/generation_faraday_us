The Generation Faraday Max Velcro offers unparalleled protection against wireless distractions, combining waterproof durability with advanced signal-blocking technology. Ideal for daily use in K-12 and higher education classrooms, this bag keeps smartphones and mobile devices completely offline, preventing access to Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, cell signals (including 5G), and more. With its patented double roll closure and velcro seal, this bag not only ensures effective shielding but also protects devices from water, dirt, and daily wear. Designed to last 4+ school years, this premium option is perfect for schools seeking a long-term, reliable solution to help students stay focused on their studies.

Specifications

  • Features Advanced Signal Blocking Technology – Faraday bags block all incoming and outgoing signals—preventing calls, texts, notifications, and even wireless headphone connections—creating a distraction-free environment for students.
  • Durable, Long-Lasting Design – Built with materials to repel water, dirt, and debris, providing protection. Designed to last 4+ school years, making it an ideal, cost-effective solution for schools.
  • Easy-to-Use Closure – The patented double roll closure, enhanced with a velcro seal, ensures that phones remain securely stored and disconnected throughout the school day. Also available with a velcro or locking closure.
  • Spacious Interior – Fits a smartphone and a smartwatch, including those with protective cases.
  • Unique RFID & Serial Number Tracking – Every bag is equipped with an RFID tag and serial number, making it instantly traceable. This enables efficient assignment to students and gives administrators clear visibility for streamlined tracking and management.
    • Built-in RFID tag can also serve as a universal student identifier, helping streamline systems like lunch, transportation, attendance, and campus access.

Usage & Care

  1. Place the smartphone inside the bag.
  2. Securely close the bag by rolling the top and using the magnetic seal.
  3. The device will be fully shielded from wireless signals and protected from water and debris.
  4. To clean, gently wipe away any residue with a soft, damp cloth. Avoid using a washing machine or heavy scrubbing to maintain bag integrity.
