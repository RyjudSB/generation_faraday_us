Generation Faraday Class Phone Organizer

Contact Us

The Generation Faraday Class Phone Organizer is a durable aluminum shelving unit compatible with all Generation Faraday Phone Bags. It features 30 compartments sized for easy access, with a design that prevents magnetic bags from sticking together during retrieval. The unit can be wall-mounted or placed on a table or floor. Predrilled side holes allow installation of Generation Faraday Unlock Docks (sold separately) when used with Max Lock bags. It can also be used without Generation Faraday Phone Bags by placing devices directly in the compartments. Engineered to keep devices organized and accessible, it supports efficient classroom management.

Specifications

  • 30-Phone Capacity – 30 phone size compartments perfectly sized to fit Generation Faraday Phone Bags.
  • Organized Layout – Allows for easy retrieval and monitoring of devices.
  • Flexible Mounting Design – Mount on a wall or use on a tabletop for versatile placement.
  • Unlock Dock Integration – Pre-drilled side holes for seamless installation of Generation Faraday Unlock Docks, enabling quick unlocking of Max Lock bags.
  • Robust Construction – Durable construction with a stable form factor measuring 35.8" L x 10" W x 20" H for reliable, long-term use.
  • SKU – GFSA-ORG

Usage & Care

  1. Placing the Class Phone Organizer – Position the Class Phone Organizer on a stable surface or mount it on a wall using the included mounting plate (additional wall installation hardware not included). Ensure it is secure and within easy reach for users.
  2. Installing Unlock Docks – If desired, use the predrilled side holes to install Generation Faraday Unlock Docks (sold separately) when used with Max Lock bags.
  3. Cleaning – Wipe the surface with a dry or slightly damp cloth. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive materials.
