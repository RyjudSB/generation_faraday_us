Generation Faraday Storage Hamper

Contact Us

The Generation Faraday Storage Hamper is a lightweight, portable, and collapsible storage solution designed to organize and transport Generation Faraday Phone Bags (or similar items) efficiently. Constructed from durable 600D polyester fabric with a removable internal pole structure, it maintains its shape while remaining easy to fold and carry. With a 170L capacity, the hamper can hold up to 100–150 phone bags—ideal for schools, events, and facilities managing large volumes. It features wheels and side handles for smooth mobility, plus mesh side pockets for added convenience. The cinch string cover keeps contents secure during transport and storage.

Specifications

  • High Capacity – 170L volume fits approximately 100–150 Generation Faraday Phone Bags.
  • Lightweight Construction – Made from 600D polyester fabric with a removable internal pole structure that maintains shape while remaining easy to fold and carry.
  • Portable Design – Equipped with four plastic wheels and two side handles for easy transport.
  • Convenient Storage – Includes two mesh side pockets for accessories or small items.
  • Collapsible Frame  – Folds flat for compact, space-saving storage when not in use.
  • Secure Closure – Cinch string cover keeps contents contained and protected.
  • Versatile Applications – Perfect for schools, events, and facilities managing high quantities of phone bags or similar items.
  • Large Dimensions – 21.7 × 17.7 × 27.6 in (55 × 45 × 70 cm)
  • SKU – GFSA-HAMPER

Usage & Care

  1. Assembly – Insert the removable poles before use to maintain full structure and shape.
  2. Loading – Distribute phone bags evenly to prevent tipping or uneven wear.
  3. Securing – Use the cinch string cover to keep contents contained during transport.
  4. Mobility – Roll using the plastic wheels on flat, smooth surfaces for best results.
  5. Cleaning – Wipe with a damp cloth or mild detergent; do not machine wash or tumble dry.
