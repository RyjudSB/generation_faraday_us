The Unlock Dock is a sleek and reliable magnetic unlocking station specifically designed for use with Generation Faraday locking bags. This compact station ensures seamless and efficient unlocking for high-traffic environments, such as schools or workplaces, where secure device management is a priority.

Specifications

  • Magnetic Unlocking Mechanism –  Simplifies the process of opening Generation Faraday locking bags, providing fast and secure access.
  • Compact and Durable Design – Crafted from high-quality materials to withstand heavy use in busy settings.
  • Non-Slip Base – Ensures stability during operation, making it easy for users to unlock bags without hassle.
  • Universal Compatibility – Designed to work seamlessly with all Generation Faraday locking bags equipped with the MAX-LOCK system.
  • Low Maintenance – Requires minimal upkeep for consistent and reliable performance.
  • SKU – GFUD-DOCK

Usage & Care

  1. Placing the Unlock Dock – Position the Unlock Dock on a stable surface or mount it as needed. Ensure it is within easy reach for users.
  2. Unlocking a Bag – Align the Generation Faraday Max-Lock phone bag with the Unlock Dock and press gently to disengage the locking mechanism.
  3. Cleaning – Wipe the surface with a dry or slightly damp cloth. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive materials.
  4. Storage – Keep in a clean, dry area when not in use. Avoid exposure to extreme heat or moisture.
  5. Security Considerations – If using in a restricted setting, consider pairing with the Tamper-Proof Cover or mounting solutions for controlled access.
