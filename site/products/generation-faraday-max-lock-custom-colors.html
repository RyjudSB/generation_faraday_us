Usage & Care

  1. Place the smartphone inside the bag.
  2. Securely close the bag by rolling the top and using the magnetic seal.
  3. Clamp the locking clasp around the metal rectangular ring.
  4. The device will be fully shielded from wireless signals and protected from water and debris.
  5. To unlock the bag, press the bottom of the locking clasp against an unlocking tool.
  6. To clean, gently wipe away any residue with a soft, damp cloth. Avoid using a washing machine or heavy scrubbing to maintain bag integrity.
