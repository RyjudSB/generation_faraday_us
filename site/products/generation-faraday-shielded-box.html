The Generation Faraday Shielded Box is the perfect solution for schools aiming to maintain distraction-free classrooms while securely storing phones. This premium signal-blocking box combines advanced shielding technology with a durable, hard-sided design to keep devices offline, protected, and securely locked. Equipped with a locking mechanism, the box ensures that phones remain safely stored during class time, preventing unauthorized access. Its advanced signal-blocking feature not only keeps devices offline but also blocks disruptive rings, dings, and notifications, fostering a focused learning environment. Made with sturdy materials and a felt-lined interior, the box prevents scratches and protects devices, while its organized storage design allows for easy identification and retrieval of phones when needed. As a reliable alternative to traditional signal-blocking methods, the Shielded Box offers superior durability, security, and accessibility, making it an essential tool for maintaining focus and minimizing distractions in modern classrooms.

Usage & Care

  1. Placing Devices in the Box - Open the lid and place devices inside. Ensure all devices are fully inside before closing the lid for complete signal blocking.
  2. Locking Capability - A lock can be installed on the lid to ensure devices remain secure.
  3. Cleaning - Wipe down with a soft, dry cloth. Avoid excessive moisture to protect the shielding material.
  4. Storage & Handling - Keep in a stable location away from any access points or routers for optimal signal blocking.
  5. Signal Blocking Check - Periodically test the box’s effectiveness by placing a phone inside and calling it to ensure no signal penetration.
