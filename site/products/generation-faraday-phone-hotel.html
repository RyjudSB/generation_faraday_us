The Generation Faraday Phone Hotel offers educators and administrators an effective solution for managing cell phones in classrooms and other learning environments. Designed to work seamlessly with Generation Faraday bags, this organizer provides a secure and organized way to store up to 35 phones, keeping them shielded from wireless signals and out of students' hands during class. Built with durability and practicality in mind, the Phone Hotel is equipped to withstand the demands of daily use in busy educational settings. Its transparent compartments allow for easy identification of devices using the ID card slots on the Faraday Bags. The organizer can be conveniently mounted using the grommets for wall installation or hung over doors with the included removable hangers, providing versatile placement options to suit any space. Ideal for classrooms, testing centers, and group study areas, the Generation Faraday Phone Hotel helps maintain focus, reduce distractions, and support a structured and secure learning environment. Its heavy-duty construction ensures long-term usability, making it an indispensable tool for enforcing cell phone policies effectively.

Usage & Care

  1. Hang the Phone Hotel on a wall or over the door.
  2. Generation Faraday bags can either stay stationed in their compartments for consistent organization or be easily removed if students need to carry them to other classes.
  3. The top of each bag extends slightly above the compartment, allowing for quick and effortless insertion or removal of devices while they remain housed in the Phone Hotel.
  4. To clean, gently wipe away any residue with a soft, damp cloth. Avoid using a washing machine or heavy scrubbing to maintain integrity.
