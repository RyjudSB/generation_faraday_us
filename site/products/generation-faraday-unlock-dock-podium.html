The Unlock Dock Podium is a durable aluminum stand available in 2-way and 4-way configurations, designed for students to efficiently unlock Generation Faraday locking bags. Featuring Unlock Dock hubs on multiple sides, it enables fast, simultaneous access to locked devices. The podium includes a sturdy base and caster wheels for easy mobility and flexible positioning. Compatible with <a href="https://generationfaraday.com/product/unlock-dock-cover/">Tamper-Proof Covers</a> (sold separately), this robust stand offers a durable, versatile solution to streamline device management.

Usage & Care

  1. Placement - Set on stable, level ground. If caster wheels are installed, lock them in place. Adjust height for comfortable reach.
  2. Unlocking a Bag - Hold the Generation Faraday Max Lock bag, align with the dock, and press until the lock releases.
  3. Cleaning - Wipe with a dry or slightly damp cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasives.
  4. Storage - Keep in a clean, dry area away from extreme heat or moisture.
  5. Security - For restricted settings, use a Tamper-Proof Cover for controlled access.
