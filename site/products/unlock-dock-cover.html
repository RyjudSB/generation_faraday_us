Generation Faraday Unlock Dock Tamper-Proof Cover

Contact Us

The Tamper-Proof Cover is a standalone accessory designed to enhance the security of the Generation Faraday Unlock Dock. This lockable cover integrates seamlessly with the Unlock Dock, preventing unauthorized access to the unlocking mechanism while maintaining a professional and durable design. Ideal for schools, workplaces, or other environments requiring controlled access to Faraday locking bags.

Request a Quote

Specifications

  • Independent Tamper-Proof Solution - Adds a layer of security to the Unlock Dock by restricting access to the unlocking mechanism.
  • Lockable Design - Features a key to ensure only authorized users can remove the cover.
  • High-Durability Materials - Crafted from tamper-resistant materials to withstand heavy use and deter unauthorized attempts.
  • Seamless Compatibility - Designed specifically to fit and function with the Generation Faraday Unlock Dock.
  • Simple Installation - Easy to attach and secure over the Unlock Dock for enhanced security without additional setup.
  • Portable and Adaptable - Can be moved and secured in different locations and installed into a variety of surfaces; mounting hardware not included.
  • SKU - GFUD-COVER

Usage & Care

  1. Placement - Position against a secure, flat surface and attach using your own mounting hardware.
  2. Attach Dock - Secure the Unlock Dock to the cover using the predrilled holes and included screws.
  3. Access - Unlock the cover and open the door to use the dock.
  4. Unlocking a Bag - Hold the Generation Faraday Max Lock bag, align with the dock, and press until the lock releases.
  5. Cleaning - Wipe with a dry or slightly damp cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasives.
  6. Storage - Keep in a clean, dry area away from extreme heat or moisture.
