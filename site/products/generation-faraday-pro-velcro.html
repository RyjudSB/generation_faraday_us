The Generation Faraday Pro is built for durability, designed to handle the rigors of daily student use in K-12 classrooms. By blocking all wireless signals, it ensures that smartphones stay disconnected during class, allowing students to stay focused on their learning. Made from water-resistant ballistic nylon and featuring two layers of high-shielding fabric, this bag is both tough and effective, ideal for schools looking for a long-term solution. Available with avelcro or a magnetic closure, the Generation Faraday Pro is designed to match the needs of the school. Designed to last for 2-4 school years, this bag is perfect for students who need a reusable and reliable way to secure their devices.

Specifications

  • Features Advanced Signal Blocking Technology – Faraday bags block all incoming and outgoing signals—preventing calls, texts, notifications, and even wireless headphone connections—creating a distraction-free environment for students.
  • Durable, School-Ready Design – Built with a water-resistant ballistic nylon exterior for long-lasting use.
  • Spacious Interior – Fits a smartphone and a smartwatch, including those with protective cases.
  • Easy-to-Use Closure – Features a double roll and velcro seal, ensuring a secure closure while allowing easy access in specified zones or where allowed.
  • Transparent ID Sleeve – Includes a clear card slot for displaying student information or Student ID.
  • Unique RFID & Serial Number Tracking – Every bag is equipped with an RFID tag and serial number, making it instantly traceable. This enables efficient assignment to students and gives administrators clear visibility for streamlined tracking and management.
    • Built-in RFID tag can also serve as a universal student identifier, helping streamline systems like lunch, transportation, attendance, and campus access.

Usage & Care

  1. Place the smartphone inside the bag.
  2. Securely close the bag by rolling the top and using the velcro seal.
  3. The device will be fully shielded from wireless signals and protected from water and debris.
  4. To clean, gently wipe away any residue with a soft, damp cloth. Avoid using a washing machine or heavy scrubbing to maintain bag integrity.
