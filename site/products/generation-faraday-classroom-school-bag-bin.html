Generation Faraday Classroom School Bag Bin

Contact Us

The Generation Faraday Classroom Storage Bin is a durable, collapsible solution designed to streamline the storage and distribution of Generation Faraday Phone Bags. Built from rigid polypropylene (PP), it maintains its structure during daily use while remaining lightweight and easy to transport. With a capacity of up to 36 phone bags (with or without devices inside), this bin helps classrooms stay organized and efficient during collection and return processes. Its open-top design allows for quick access, while integrated handles make it easy to move between classrooms or storage areas. Ideal for schools, events, and facilities managing large volumes of phone bags, this bin offers a simple, space-saving way to keep devices secure and organized throughout the day.

Specifications

  • 36-Bag Capacity – Holds up to 36 Generation Faraday phone bags (empty or with devices inside).
  • Universal Compatibility – Works with all Generation Faraday phone bag models.
  • Lightweight and Portable – Easy to carry with two integrated side handles.
  • Collapsible Design – Folds flat for compact storage when not in use.
  • Durable Construction – Made from rigid PP plastic for long-term, daily use in school environments.
  • Built for Daily Use – Designed to handle the weight of fully loaded phone bags with devices without warping or collapsing.
  • Open Access Design – Wide, unobstructed top for quick loading and retrieval.
  • Ventilated Mesh Sides – Allows visibility of contents for easy inventory checks.
  • Dimensions – 17.3 × 13.0 × 9.1 in (44 × 33 × 23 cm)
  • Weight – 2.1 lb (970 g)
  • SKU – GFSA-BIN

Usage & Care

  1. Insert Bags – Place Generation Faraday phone bags into the bin for organized storage and distribution.
  2. Transport as Needed – Use the built-in handles to move the bin between classrooms or storage areas.
  3. Collapse for Storage – Fold down the side walls to store flat when not in use.
  4. Care – Clean with a soft, damp cloth as needed.
