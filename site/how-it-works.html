Questions & Answers

Why choose Faraday bags over other classroom management options?

Faraday bags are a simple, cost-effective solution that completely blocks distractions by cutting off all signals so that phones do not vibrate or ding. Unlike other options, they ensure that students retain their devices, solving the question of possession and allowing for access in case of emergency.

How do Faraday bags help in classrooms?

Faraday bags are a tool for teachers and administrators to maintain a focused learning environment by blocking  the signals to students’ devices. This helps keep attention on classwork, free from the distractions of notifications, texts, and social media.

How exactly do Faraday bags block signals?

Faraday bags use advanced signal-blocking materials that create a barrier around electronic devices, effectively “silencing” them by preventing wireless signals from coming in or going out

Are all types of signals blocked in a Faraday bag?

Faraday bags block various signals, including Wi-Fi, Cellular, and Bluetooth. This comprehensive coverage keeps devices offline, helping students avoid digital temptations while in class.

Which devices should be placed in Faraday bags?

Devices like smartphones, tablets, smartwatches, and small laptops that connect to the internet or use wireless communication are ideal candidates for Generation Faraday bags. Keeping these devices offline helps ensure a distraction-free learning experience.

How do Faraday bags make classroom management easier?

By keeping devices offline, Generation Faraday bags minimize disruptions and help students stay present. This allows teachers to focus more on teaching and less on managing digital distractions in the classroom.

Can Faraday bags be used repeatedly?

Absolutely! Our Generation Faraday bags are built for frequent use and can handle daily wear and tear. They’re a long-lasting solution for managing devices in educational settings

In an emergency, can students access their phones if they’re in a Faraday bag?

Yes, in any emergency, students can quickly remove their phones from the bag, restoring full connectivity. Teachers understand the importance of access in these situations and can assist as needed.

What happens in case of an emergency if phones are stored in Faraday bags?

Schools have protocols in place for emergencies. Instructors can allow students to retrieve their phones if needed, ensuring that important communication can occur without delay.

Can accommodations be made for students with special circumstances who need access to their phones?

 Yes, exceptions can be made for students who require phone access due to medical needs or other special situations. Families can coordinate with the school to arrange any necessary accommodations.

How do the locking bags work?

Faraday Max-Lock requires a specific magnetic key to unlock it. This keeps the phones protected from unauthorized access and allows for staff to to unlock them when required.
