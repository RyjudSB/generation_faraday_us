Powerful Tools for Policy Enforcement

Key Features of The Generation Faraday App

Asset Tracking & Integrated Setup

The Generation Faraday App streamlines inventory management by tracking Faraday bags across schools and districts, flagging lost or stolen items, and ensuring seamless deployment. Each bag’s serial number—supported by RFID tagging—is tied to an individual student, allowing administrators to monitor compliance and quickly address any issues. With setup included from day one, schools gain real-time tracking and enforcement in a single, easy-to-use platform.

Compliance Monitoring

The Generation Faraday App helps enforce phone policies by identifying students who are not using their bags for follow-up. Administrators can monitor classrooms in real time, ensuring adherence and addressing compliance issues as they arise.

School-Level Insights

School-level insights provide a comprehensive view of deployment and compliance trends, making it easier for administrators to track policy effectiveness. By aggregating data into our intuitive dashboards, the app enables school leaders to identify patterns, address challenges, and make informed decisions to improve compliance and support learning environments.

Access Anywhere, Anytime

The Generation Faraday App isn’t just for mobile—it’s also available on desktop for seamless access wherever you are. Easily track compliance from your computer or on the go, ensuring a smooth and efficient experience for teachers and administrators. User Access Hierarchy

A Layered Approach to Compliance Monitoring

Understanding how different roles interact with the app is key to effective monitoring and compliance.

Districts provide an overarching view, with aggregate reporting and oversight across multiple schools, allowing for district-wide analysis and decision-making.

Teachers and Admins have the ability to monitor compliance within their specific classrooms, ensuring students follow policies.

Students are tracked individually, with their device usage monitored through the app.
