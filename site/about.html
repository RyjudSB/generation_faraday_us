Our Story

Generation Faraday, a division of Mission Darkness, is backed by an established brand known for supplying military and police forces, possibly even in your local area. Designed with the same cutting-edge technology, Generation Faraday was created to tackle a crucial issue. Utilizing signal-blocking technology, Generation Faraday is committed to fostering distraction-free, focused learning and social environments. Without signal-blocking technology, distractions persist—other brands may secure your phone, but they don’t secure the signal. Distractions don’t stop unless the signal stops. By helping schools and communities enforce effective cell phone use policies, the company aims to mitigate digital distractions, promote mindfulness, and support the mental and emotional well-being of students.
