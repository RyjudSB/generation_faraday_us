How do Generation Faraday Bags work?

Generation Faraday Bags use advanced signal-blocking materials to create an electromagnetic shield, preventing devices inside from sending or receiving any wireless communication, including cellular, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth signals. This helps minimize distractions and fosters a focused learning environment.

Do Faraday bags block all wireless signals?

Yes, Generation Faraday Bags block Wi-Fi, cellular, and Bluetooth signals, ensuring that devices remain completely offline while inside the bag.

Will I receive calls, texts, notifications, or emergency alerts while my phone is in the Faraday bag?

No. The Faraday bag blocks all incoming and outgoing wireless communication, including calls, texts, app notifications, and government-issued emergency alerts such as Amber Alerts or severe weather warnings. These alerts will only appear once the phone is removed from the bag and reconnects to the network.

Can students still hear alarms or notifications while their phones are in the Faraday bag?

Yes. While the bag blocks all wireless signals, it does not disable a phone’s internal functions. Alarms, timers, and pre-downloaded notifications may still sound or vibrate if the phone is not set to silent.

Which devices should be placed in a Faraday bag?

Smartphones, tablets, smartwatches, and small laptops that use wireless communication should be placed in Generation Faraday Bags to maintain a distraction-free environment.

Can I test if my Faraday bag is working?

Yes! Place your phone inside the bag, seal it, and try calling or sending a message. If the phone does not receive a signal, the bag is working properly. Benefits and Use in Schools

Why choose Faraday bags over other classroom management options?

Faraday bags are a simple, cost-effective solution that fully blocks signals, eliminating all distractions—no notifications, rings, or vibrations to interrupt focus. Unlike competitor options, which may only limit access, Faraday bags ensure that students retain possession of their phones while staying completely engaged in class, with access available in emergencies.

How do Faraday bags help in classrooms?

By blocking digital distractions like notifications, texts, and social media, Faraday bags help teachers and administrators maintain a focused learning environment.

How do Faraday bags make classroom management easier?

Keeping devices offline reduces disruptions, allowing teachers to focus on instruction rather than managing digital distractions.

Why not just have students turn their phones off instead of using a Faraday bag?

Turning phones off is an option, but it relies on students complying—and phones can be quickly turned back on. Faraday bags ensure phones stay completely disconnected, even if powered on, preventing cheating, messaging, or app use during class. To avoid audible disruptions, schools can require silent mode before bagging, but the bags provide the critical guarantee of signal isolation that powering off alone does not.
